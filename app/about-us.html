About us

We cater across and around London & nearby counties

Foodsy was started by Yulia after spending 3 years as a chef at a private member’s club and 3 years working with a number of leading catering companies. Her passion was to offer a reasonably priced catering without compromising quality… the result is a long list of very happy customers!

​Foodsy is a London based caterer striving to deliver delicious menus & amazing experiences for all party occasions, incorporating great food with stylish presentation and friendly, well-trained staff.

We are here to take the hassle out of entertaining & help you with every aspect of your party planning. From the preliminary stages of choosing your menu to the end product; we will help you every step of the way.

We offer our service for all types of events and whatever the numbers

  • Ready to Serve party food delivered direct to your door for smaller home or office parties
  • Private Chef Service for corporate lunches & private dinners
  • Course a Full Catering Service with waiting staff
  • Equipment hire for bigger private & corporate events, weddings & venue catering.

We offer all types of menus

  • Canapés receptions
  • Stylish dinners
  • Substantial bowl food
  • Buffet catering
  • Summer barbecues
  • Bespoke menus
Take the time to view our menu selections which capture the flavours and colours of the seasons. We hope you will get in touch!
